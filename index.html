About Us

Dr. E. Khalieraaj

Cybersecurity Leader | Academic Innovator | Social Advocate

Dr. E. Khalieraaj brings a unique combination of corporate and academic expertise, with a primary focus on cybersecurity and marketing. His career spans over 10 years, during which he has led national projects, published influential research, and conducted awareness programs across 200+ institutions, impacting over 100,000 students. As the Project Head for the "National Cyber Crime Reference Handbook," his contributions have helped shape India's cybersecurity landscape.

Dr. Khalieraaj holds a Doctorate in Cyber Security from the University of Swahili, Panama, and multiple prestigious degrees in business, public relations, and computer science. His leadership extends beyond academia as he plays pivotal roles in the MSME Connect Initiative (Ministry of MSME, Govt. of India), the National Youth Council of India, and the Indo-Russia IT Association, where he serves as Vice President of the Cyber Security Division.

Our Mission

Dr. E. Khalieraaj aims to strengthen cybersecurity in India through awareness, education, and innovative solutions, with a focus on protecting women, children, and vulnerable groups.

Our Vision

The aim is to position India as a global cybersecurity leader by ensuring digital safety and empowering citizens to protect themselves in a connected world.
